Technical Quiz

Rules :

• Only team entries are eligible. The students of different colleges may form a team.

• Only team entries are eligible. The students of different colleges may form a team.

• Mobiles are not allowed to use while participation.

• Final judgement will be made by judges/event co-ordinators based on performance.

• Team members should register individually.

• Candidates should bring their college id card at the time of participation.

Level 1 :(Elimination)

• Each team would be given a question paper containing 30 objective type questions.

• Each question contains one mark.

• No negative marking.

• Allotted time for answering the questions is 20 minutes.

• Only top 10 teams are selected for LEVEL 2.

Level 2 :(Round 1)

• Each team would be given a question paper containing 30 objective type questions.

• Each question contains one mark.

• No negative marking.

• Allotted time for answering the questions is 20 minutes.

• Only top 10 teams are selected for LEVEL 2.

Level 2 :(Round 2)

• Each team must be asked 5 questions individually.

• No negative marking and pass option is allowed.

• Time limit for discussion is 1 minute.

• Based on their marks only top 5 teams are allowed for next round.

Round 2 :(Final)

• It is a mark based questions round.

• Each team must be asked 5 questions individually.

• Each team will give a chance to select to 4 marks, 5 marks, 6 marks Questions.

• Allotted time for team discussion is 2 minutes.

• Tie breaker question will be asked in case any two teams get same marks.
