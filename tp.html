Technical Paper Presentation

Rules :

1. Candidates should send their paper in soft copy to our mail id: beccivilevents@gmail.com

2. Accepted papers will be intimated through their respective mail id.

3. All accepted papers based on their merit given an opportunity to present orally.

4. The time for presentation is 8 minutes and for queries 2 minutes.

5. Selected candidates should bring their identity card at the time of presentation.

6. Selected candidates should bring two hard copies and one soft copy of the paper at the time of presentation.

7. Only LCD projectors will be provided.

8. Paper should be submitted as a PDF or word document.

9. Bring your Soft Copies in Pen drive.

10. Details of the team members: Name, Department, E-Mail ID, Contact number, Institution name should be sent along with the Paper.

11. A maximum of four members are allowed in a team.

12. The last date for the submission of soft copy through E-mail ID is on or before 5:00PM 28th Feb 2023.
