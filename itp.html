Technical Paper Presentation

Rules :

• Only undergraduate students are allowed to send in their entries.

• A team can present only one paper.

• If the paper is co-authored then there can be only 2 members at max.( one author and the other co-author).

• The Oral presentation should be in the form of Microsoft power point slides which contains the brief of your paper.

• The presenters should bring power point slides in a pen drive.

• The presentation should last a maximum of 7 minutes. There will be a 3 minutes query and answer session following it.

• The presenters should be punctual for their presentation as scheduled and register with the respective session chair before their paper presentation session.

• The presenters should be in their formal attire, college ID card during the presentation.

• The presenters should submit a hard copy of the paper in A4 sheets with a cover page specifying the title of the paper, names of authors, college names, contact numbers and email ids.

• The Best Paper Award is under the judge and the organizers discretion.

Guidlines :

• The topic should be on recent advances in the field of Computer Science and Technology.

• Abstract should not exceed more than 250 words and Paper should not exceed 10 pages.

• Paper should be in IEEE Format.

• Paper must be preceded by a cover page specifying the title of the paper, names of authors and their college names as also their contact numbers and email ids.

• Based on the peer review of the paper, few papers will be nominated for presenting the paper.
