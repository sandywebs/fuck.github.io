Technical Cross Word

Rules :

1. Maximum no.of students in a team is 2

2. A crossword puzzle consists of a grid, with each box in the grid designated to one letter.

3. The clues are numbered from 1 across and 1 down, to signify which direction the word goes.

4. When completing a crossword puzzle, the goal is to solve every clue and enter every letter and word on the grid.

5. You can solve the clues in any order.

6. The clues given to you are only technical words.

7. Participants are not be allowed to use mobile or any other electronic gadgets during the contest.
